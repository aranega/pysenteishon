Pysenteishon

Gives you skillz to let you change your presentation slides with your smartphone!

About Pysenteishon

Its a simple Python application that let you control your slide presentations just swiping your finger at your cellphone touchscreen.

How it works

Its a web based application that listen by default on port 5000. When you connect to this service with your smartphone (using a standard web browser), your finger swipes are captured by the cool javascript library TouchSwipe. Then, thanks to the excellent PyUserInput python library, each javascript swipe event calls a specific url that emulates a keyboard tap.

How to use it

Run pysenteishon in your laptop. If you have a firewall running, be sure to allow connections on port 5000. Then open your presentation with the software you used to, be sure the application has focus. In your smartphone, open the url given by pysenteishon. Swipe your finger at the touchscreen to control your slides and start your talk/presentation! For further instructions, click on the following ninja assistant >>>
